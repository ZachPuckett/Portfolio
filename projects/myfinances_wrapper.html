UX Design for Financial Literacy: MyFinancesUW

This project addressed financial literacy challenges among young adults by designing an interactive, web-based tool that guides users through goal-setting and financial learning. The prototype was developed in Figma and emphasized personalization and usability.

The system was designed for college students and featured onboarding, AI support, and dynamic dashboards tailored to individual financial needs. The design process included wireframing, moodboarding, and high-fidelity prototyping supported by user research and testing.

  • User research included surveys and interviews with college students to assess gaps in financial knowledge and preferred learning modes.
  • Customized dashboards were created based on a quiz assessing users’ financial goals, enabling a tailored resource and task-tracking experience.
  • AI-powered ChatBot functionality allowed users to ask financial questions and receive guidance for goal completion and site navigation.
  • Interface elements such as dropdown menus, icons, and progress trackers were implemented based on usability feedback to enhance navigability and engagement.
