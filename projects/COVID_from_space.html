Tracking COVID-19 Impacts in Spain from Space

This project visualizes changes in nighttime light intensity across Spain and Portugal using VIIRS data from Google Earth Engine, revealing the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic on urban and rural lighting patterns.

I used difference imaging to isolate change in light emissions across two intervals: 2015–2020 (pre-to-during COVID) and 2020–2023 (during-to-post COVID). Through these periods, Lisbon and Porto revealed dramatic fluctuations, while Madrid displayed strong post-pandemic resurgence in activity.

  • Used Google Earth Engine to import and filter VIIRS Stray Light Corrected monthly composites.
  • Created radiance difference images by subtracting pixel values across years to track pre- and post-COVID changes.
  • Applied customized visualization palettes and developed legends with UI panels for each output map.
  • Exported processed imagery for analysis, identifying areas with increased, decreased, or unchanged nighttime activity.

The results suggest urban coastal areas like Lisbon experienced stricter lockdown responses compared to Madrid, while rural interiors remained relatively unaffected. This difference in regulatory intensity and socioeconomic activity is clearly expressed in the satellite data.

2015–2020 (Before COVID)

VIIRS Stray Light Corrected Nighttime Day/Night Band Composites Version 1 dataset, difference in nighttime light intensity between 2015 and 2020 over Spain and Portugal. Scale = 1000. Blue (3A33FF) indicates a decrease in nighttime light intensity. White (FFFFFF) represents no significant change. Red (FF3333) highlights areas where nighttime light intensity increased.

2020–2023 (After COVID)

VIIRS Stray Light Corrected Nighttime Day/Night Band Composites Version 1 dataset, difference in nighttime light intensity between 2020 and 2023 over Spain and Portugal. Scale = 1000. Blue (3A33FF) indicates a decrease in nighttime light intensity. White (FFFFFF) represents no significant change. Red (FF3333) highlights areas where nighttime light intensity increased.
